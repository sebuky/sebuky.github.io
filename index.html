SEBUKY

Folktronic

Born in Ecuador, rised in Barcelona. Sebuky researches and creates with musical instruments and rhythms from around the world but particularly from South America, adding electronic .. beats enriched with algorithms that provide randomness to sound effects thanks to his other profession, computer science.

SHOW FORMATS

Live Set

Each concert is a different experience, it includes Andean wind instruments (quenas, pinguyos, sicus) as well as electronic controllers and depending on the conditions of the place can also include: Marimba de Chonta, guitar, charango, Galician bagpipes.

Dj Set

Dj with a set with musicians and producers I know or I admire.. can be an Hybrid Set if I use some flutes.

Live Band

In Live Band mode* musicians of Latin american roots join to enrich the show with live voice, sound effects and Latin folk percussion (Colombian, Peruvian and Argentinian .. ) *currently available in Europe only
