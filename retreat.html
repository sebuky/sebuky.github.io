Our Intention

This retreat is an invitation to step away from the pace of everyday life and enter a space where you can listen more deeply to yourself. Through a blend of yoga, movement, music, meditation, and shared moments, we create an environment that supports genuine exploration, presence, and inner connection.

Our intention is to hold a space that feels welcoming, respectful, and supportive — a place where you can simply be as you are. The practices and workshops are designed to gently guide you inward, helping you reconnect with your body, your creativity, and the quiet wisdom within.

At the same time, we believe that integration happens not only during structured sessions but also in the spaces in between. The schedule is intentionally balanced, offering meaningful experiences alongside generous free time to rest, enjoy the pool, walk in nature, or treat yourself to a restorative massage.

Together we will create a small, harmonious community for a few days — sharing practices, meals, laughter, and silence — allowing space for both personal reflection and authentic connection with others.

If you feel the desire to pause, breathe, and reconnect with yourself in a supportive and inspiring environment, we would be honored to share this experience with you.

The Space

Vell Can Gironès is a beautifully restored 15th-century countryside house located in Santa Llogaia del Terri, a small village between Banyoles and Girona. Surrounded by nature, it is an ideal place to disconnect from the busyness of daily life, rest, and gently reconnect with yourself.

The retreat takes place at the Mamakilla association, a peaceful and welcoming space immersed in forests, open fields, and the soft sound of a nearby river. The land offers plenty of space to slow down and breathe — with a large garden, quiet corners to relax, and nature paths to explore.

The property has a beautiful wooden yoga shala, filled with natural light and views of the surrounding greenery, as well as multiple outside spaces where we will gather for our practices, movement sessions, and workshops. In the garden, you can enjoy the swimming pool, perfect for cooling down during warm summer days, as well as many shaded spots in the garden to read, journal, or simply rest. The peaceful rural setting invites a natural rhythm of slowing down, being outdoors, and reconnecting with the body and the senses.

How to Arrive

How to arrive from Barcelona / Girona

Exit the AP-7 motorway at Exit 6 and head towards Banyoles–Olot (C-66). Then take the Cornellà del Terri exit. At the roundabout, follow the direction to Medinyà and continue for about 2–3 km until you find a junction on the left signposted “Sta. Llogaia del Terri.” When you reach the first group of houses, you should park in the free parking area of Vell Can Gironès–Mamakilla, which is signposted and will be on your right.

To arrive by public transport, take a train to Girona (train information: http://www.renfe.com/CA/viajeros/index.html ). From the same train station, take a bus operated by Teisa towards Banyoles (bus routes: http://www.teisa-bus.com/ca/rutes ). The bus runs every hour and stops at Cornellà del Terri. Once you arrive in Cornellà del Terri, arrange a pickup.

There is also the option of a taxi service (contact us for more information).
