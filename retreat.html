Return to Yourself

A 4-day embodiment retreat in the Catalan countryside

26–29 August • 4 days / 3 nights

The Experience

During this intimate late-summer retreat in the peaceful Catalan countryside, we will create a space for presence, creativity, and authentic connection — with ourselves, with others, and with nature.

During the retreat you will experience:

  • gentle morning yoga and meditation
  • embodied movement and somatic exploration
  • conscious dance journeys with live music
  • cacao and sharing circles
  • time to rest, swim, or explore nature
  • nourishing vegetarian meals
  • a supportive and intimate group atmosphere.

Location

Vell Can Gironès is a restored 15th-century countryside house located in Santa Llogaia del Terri, a small village between Banyoles and Girona. Surrounded by nature, it is an ideal place to disconnect, rest and reconnect, immersed in forests, fields and the gentle sound of the nearby river. The retreat takes place at the Mamakilla association.

About us:

This retreat is guided by Sanne, a holistic therapist, bodyworker and facilitator of movement and conscious spaces. Together with Sebuky — multi-instrumentalist and music producer — they create immersive experiences that weave together movement, sound, and authentic connection.
